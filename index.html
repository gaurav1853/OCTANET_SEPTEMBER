VARIETIES

Amber

Golden-hued, sweet, and crisp, a delightful autumn treat and becomes fully ripe in mid-October. It is mostly grown in Shopian and Kulgam.

Plump and vibrant red, strawberries are a delightful blend of sweetness and tartness, often enjoyed fresh, in desserts, or as a topping.

pineapple

Tropical and tropical, pineapples offer a juicy and tropical flavor that adds a zesty twist to dishes and drinks, while also packing vitamins and enzymes.

Banana

Creamy and potassium-rich, bananas are a convenient and energy-boosting fruit with a naturally sweet taste, perfect for on-the-go or as a smoothie base.

Small and succulent, grapes come in clusters and offer a sweet and juicy burst of flavor. They are enjoyed as a snack, in salads, or as a refreshing addition to fruit platters and wine production.
